Personal Life

Born in Paris, France, I quickly moved to the United States at the young age of 2. After three years, I moved back to Paris. After three more years, I returned to the US. After Three more years, I moved to London. After only one year, I moved to Boulder, Colorado and have been in the state ever since. Living between these two worlds, I was given an opportunity to understand how people change around our planet.

Chef's Life

While living in Boulder I worked at multiple different restaurants, but one of these restaurants made a huge impact on my professional life. I worked as a Garm Chef at a restaurant called Pizzeria Locale, or now, Pizzeria Alberico. This was such a life changing experience because during my time there, I had the chance to be guided by chefs at Pizzeria Alberico’s sister restaurant. These chefs changed the way I now see food, the way I see any tedious task, and the way I interact with coworkers. This sister restaurant, Frasca Food and Wine, is now the only place in Boulder to have earned a Michelin Star. Being able to work in this professional environment during my high school career was really what made me realize that through hard work, we can achieve great things.
