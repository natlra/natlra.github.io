2023 Coaster Project

CNC Vertical Mill Certification

Project Overview

The Coaster Project is a part of the HAAS CNC vertical mill operator certification and is designed to help users become more familiar with the operating controls of the CNC mill. This project uses lightweight aerospace design to create a unique coaster.

Cost Estimate

Producing the clock parts involves costs associated with materials, machine time, and finishing processes. A detailed cost estimate ensures effective project budgeting and resource allocation.

  • Aluminum Raw Stock: $7.89
  • Total Material Cost: $7.89
  • Labor Cost: $24.77 x ~1 hours = $24.77
  • Total Individual Clock Cost: $32.66

Reducing Manufacturing Costs

By utilizing different manufacturing techniques, we can effectively reduce the cost of production for these coasters. Here are some examples:

  • Buy bulk raw stock to reduce individual material cost.
  • Match the demand curve with supply.
  • Change the design to save time while cutting material.

Other Comments

Unfortunately, the lightweight design of the coaster is impractical for mass manufacturing and does not function properly. These triangular holes are very visually appealing but let moisture follow the surface from the beverage to the table. The design of a coaster is supposed to isolate the beverage cup from the table, but with these holes present, this specific coaster design fails at being an effective coaster.
